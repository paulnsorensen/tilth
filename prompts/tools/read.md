Read files with smart auto-sizing. Example: `tilth_read(paths: ["src/lib.rs", "src/mcp.rs#tool_search"], mode: "auto")` returns full small files, hash-prefixed signatures for large code, and sliced suffix reads when requested. Suffix grammar: `path#n-m`, `path#n`, `path### Heading`, `path#symbol_name`. Use `mode: "full"` to force full content and `mode: "signature"` for hash-prefixed signature lines.
